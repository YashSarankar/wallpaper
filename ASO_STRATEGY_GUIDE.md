## 2. ‚≠ê Early Ratings & Reviews (The Algorithm Booster)
Google loves momentum. If you get 0 downloads in the first week, your rank drops.

*   **The "First 50" Strategy:**
    *   Before you post on social media, ask 10-20 friends/family to download it and leave a **5-star review**.
    *   **Keywords in Reviews:** Ask them to write valid sentences like "Best *AMOLED wallpaper app* I've used" or "Great *4K backgrounds*." Google indexes keywords in reviews too!
    *   **Reply to Reviews:** Reply to *every single review* in the console. It shows activity.
